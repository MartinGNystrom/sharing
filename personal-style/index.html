CTI Briefing · Weekly Signal 09 Jul 2026 · WWT Internal

Access brokers are pricing MSP credentials faster than defenders can rotate them

Bottom line

Underground marketplaces are listing verified remote-access credentials for mid-market MSPs within 48 hours of compromise — down from a nine-day average in 2025. Treat credential-rotation SLAs as a control, not a formality.

Three broker forums tracked by the practice show consistent undercutting on RMM-tool access, with sellers now bundling stolen credentials with disabled-MFA confirmation. The shift tracks with a broader move toward initial-access specialization — fewer generalist listings, more verified, ready-to-use footholds.

Affiliates shortening dwell time post-purchase Medium Validate EDR coverage on MSP-managed endpoints
